Committed to seeing our clients thrive throughout the 21st century

Located in Lakewood CO, we have been supporting our clients and enabling them to continue doing what they do best - caring for their patients and surrounding communities - since 1998. We operate on the basic principal of building meaningful and lasting client relationships, providing outstanding customer service, and supporting our clients in actualizing their financial goals.

As early-out specialists, we offer a variety of services to the healthcare industry that are client specific and designed to resolve accounts receivable issues. Simply, we believe revenue goals can be met and consistently exceeded without sacrificing values or community relationships. By combining innovative programs, data driven workflows and highly trained staff with state-of-the-art technology, we help position our clients at the pinnacle of healthcare financial management.
