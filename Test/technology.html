Technology at HON

HON capitalizes on the power of technology to work smarter and faster for our clients and to produce the consistent, accurate and timely results on which we have built our reputation. HON is constantly evaluating technologies as they emerge and implementing new solutions that maintain our stated goal of operating on the forefront of new technologies. This ensures our systems meet and exceed our continued growth requirements and those of our clients.

Differentation

We believe our technology is an asset that truly sets us apart. We ensure our systems are the most sophisticated, dependable and user-friendly programs available. We understand that successful healthcare operations need to always embrace new ideas, new solutions and new technological advancements to strategically stay ahead.

Components

Everything from our operating system, to our telephone system, to our patient and client web portal, to our reporting and analytics is state-of -the-art. A few highlights of our technology are highlighted below.

Our system features a powerful and sophisticated workflow management tool we customize to uniquely fit a client's needs. What makes our technology systems unique is the customization we have implemented. We have invested in integrating the system with our telephone system, and other systems to ensure a fully integrated and seamless information flow. Instead of manipulating our operations to accommodate our system, we manipulate the system to accommodate our operations and the needs of our clients.
